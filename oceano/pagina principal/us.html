ABOUT US...

Explanation of our project


            We are an organization of students which emphasizes marine life. We look after
            several problems oceans are facing nowadays, such as plastic pollution, focus on it
            and try to come up with solutions.

Our Objective

            Our main purpose is to engage the majority of the population to do what is best for
            our oceans and the whole world. To make awareness of the problems it is and we are 
            facing, and change thoughts on this.
