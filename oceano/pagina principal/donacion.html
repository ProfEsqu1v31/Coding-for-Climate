Companies And Donations
How can we help?

These are some companies that help reduce the impact of plastic pollution 

            The Ocean Cleanup is a nonprofit environmental
            engineering organization based in the Netherlands 
            that develops technology to extract plastic pollution 
            from the oceans and to capture it in rivers before it 
            can reach the ocean.
        
            The 5 Gyres Institute is a 501 non-profit
            organization that focuses on reducing plastics
            pollution by focusing on primary research. 
            Programs concentrate on science, education 
            and adventure.
        
           Oceana, inc. is a 501 nonprofit ocean 
           conservation organization focused on 
           influencing specific policy decisions 
           on the national level to preserve and 
           restore the world's oceans.
