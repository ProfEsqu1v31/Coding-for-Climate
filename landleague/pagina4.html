Transgenics have attracted attention because of the way in which they pose to face the problem, this is referred to as organisms which have been altered by the introduction of artificial means of one or more sequences of foreign DNA from another species. Transgenic organisms are generated in a laboratory for research purposes, they can be altered so as not to be affected by pests and they can also improve the quality of the product. Argentina is one of the 5 countries in which GM products make up 90% of agriculture, along with the United States, China, Brazil and Canada.
