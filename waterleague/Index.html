Coding for Climate

Coding for climate is a project made to bring education and technology together through a global collaboration experience for classrooms. Classrooms will be introduced to computer science foundations then will use skills of problem-solving, computational thinking, creativity, and digital literacy to create solutions for our planet.

Water League

The Coding for Climate project explores different topics, which can be found classified in four different leagues: Water, Ocean, Earth and Energy. For this site, we explore an issue related to the Water League. It is of great importance to create awarness of the unimaginable amount of problems water shortage and pollution create for entire communities and the enviorment.
